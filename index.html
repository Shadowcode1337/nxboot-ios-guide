What is NXBoot?

NXBoot is an app developed for IOS and macOS to load payloads on your already in rcm booted switch, if you do not know how to get your switch into rcm follow this.

Requirements

  • A jailbroken Iphone

  • IOS 10.0+

  • Cydia

Hardware requirements

  • A Lightning OTG adapter
