What is NXBoot?

NXBoot is an app developed for IOS and macOS to start custom boot code trough the Fusee Gelee hardware exploit.

Requirements

  • A jailbroken iPhone
  • IOS 10.0+
  • a Package manager (cydia/zebra/sileo)

Hardware requirements

  • A Lightning OTG adapter
  • A USB C Cable

Instructions

  • Section 1

    • 1. Open Cydia then go to the "Sources" tab.

    2. Press edit on the top right, then click add and enter this url: https://mologie.github.io/repo/


    3. Press "Mologie".



    4. Press "All Packages".



    5. Click on NXBoot and Install it on the top right corner.

    6. After installation you should see the nxboot app on your homescreen.

    7. Open the app and click "select bootcode".

    8. Press "New Profile".

    9. After that select your payload.

    Method 1

    1. If you have iCloud,just put the payload you want to use into your iCloud files and select it.

    Method 2

    1. Install the app called "Documents by Readdle" from the App Store.

    2. After installing,open iTunes on your pc.

    3. There could be a pop up saying your iPhone can be updated! Click Cancel.

    4. Connect your iPhone to your pc and click on the little phone icon.

    5. Now go to the tab "File sharing" and select "Documents by Readdle".

    6. Drag the payload into the boxes.

    Go back to documents and go into the "iTunes Files folder.

    Copy the paylod to the "iCloud" folder.

    Then select the payload in the NXBoot app.

    7. Done!

  • Section 2

    • 1. Put your switch into rcm.

    2. After that connect your switch with the iPhone.

    3. Select your boot code.

    4. Go into the main menu of NXBoot and Press "Boot now"
